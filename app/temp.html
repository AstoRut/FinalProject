Chicago

New York is my favorite city in the world, hands down. But Chicago shares a lot of similarities with New York, though it's smaller (and cleaner). While there's definitely less going on in Chicago, it has its own allure. With a vast array of tourist attractions and diverse neighbors with their own unique charm, Chicago is a place I'd highly recommend for people looking to explore a new city. Maybe just steer clear of the winter, for obvious reasons!

Recommended Attractions

  • Lincoln Park Zoo
  • Millennium Park & Cloudgate
  • Willis Tower
  • River Cruise
